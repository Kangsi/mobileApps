Running man

  • Running Man is a South Korean variety show; a part of SBS's Good Sunday lineup, along with K-pop Star 4. Running Man was first aired on July 11, 2010. This show is classified as an "urban action variety"; a never-before-seen new genre of variety shows focused in an urban environment. The MCs and guests complete missions in a landmark to win the race. The show has since shifted to a more familiar reality-variety show concept focused on games. It has garnered attention as being the come-back program for Yoo Jae-suk, the main MC of the program, after leaving Good Sunday's Family Outing in February 2010.
